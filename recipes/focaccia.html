How to Make Focaccia Bread

Lets learn how to make some delicious focaccia bread. Focaccia is an excelent bread
to make sandwiches with mozzarella cheese and tomatoes. It is also a great bread
for garlic bread with olive oil and garlic.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of active dry yeast
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 2 cups of warm water
  • 4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • olive oil
  • rosemary

    • Steps

    1. In a small bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in 2 cups of warm water. Let stand until the mixture is frothy with bubbes and smells slightly of fermentation. (about 10 minutes).
    2. In a large bowl stir in flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture.
    3. Mix in some olive oil and press dough together with your hands. As your mixing the dough rosemary leaves.
    4. Lightly oil a large bowl, then place dough in the bowl and turn to coat with oil.
    5. Cover with a damp cloth and let rise in the refrigerator for 24 hours or up to 48 hours.
    6. Remove the bread from the refrigerator the morning you decide to bake so that the dough can wrm and flow more easily inthe baking panPunch down the dough and turn it out onto a lightly oiled surface.
    7. Brush with olive oil and sprinkle with garlic powder and additional rosemary leaves and salt flakes.
    8. preheat the oven to 400 deg farenheit and bake for 20 minutes until the surface o the bread is golden
